Delete Your Account

dots – Account Deletion

1 Overview

The dots app allows users to delete their account directly from within the app. This page explains the process and what happens to your data after deletion.

2 How to Delete Your Account

  • 1 Open the dots app
  • 2 Go to your Profile / Settings
  • 3 Tap "Delete Account"
  • 4 Confirm the deletion request

3 What Happens When You Delete

  • Your account profile will be deleted
  • Your personal account data will be removed
  • Your app activity associated with the account will be deleted or anonymized where applicable
This action is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure you want to proceed before confirming.

4 Data Retention

Some limited data may be temporarily retained for legal, security, fraud prevention, or technical compliance purposes, if required by applicable law.
