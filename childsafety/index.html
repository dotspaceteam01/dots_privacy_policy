Child Safety Standards

Last updated: April 2026

1 Zero Tolerance Policy

At dots, we are committed to ensuring a safe environment for all users and have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) or child sexual abuse material (CSAM).

dots strictly prohibits any content or behavior that involves:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
  • Exploitation of minors
  • Grooming or predatory behavior
Any such activity will result in immediate account suspension and reporting to relevant authorities.

2 User Feedback and Reporting

Currently, users cannot directly report other users or content. However, users can raise concerns, including child safety issues, through the in-app Settings → Feedback (Talk to the builders) section. All submissions are reviewed promptly by our team.

If a valid concern is identified, our team will take immediate action, including restricting or permanently blocking the reported user account and escalating the matter where necessary.

3 Content Moderation

We actively monitor and moderate user-generated content using:

  • Automated detection systems
  • Manual review processes

4 Cooperation with Law Enforcement

dots complies with all applicable child safety laws and works with regional and national law enforcement agencies when required.

5 Prevention Measures

We take steps to prevent misuse of our platform, including:

  • Account monitoring
  • Restricting suspicious activity
  • Enforcing strict community guidelines
